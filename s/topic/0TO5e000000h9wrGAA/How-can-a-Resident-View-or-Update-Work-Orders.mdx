On the **Work Order Details** page, you can view the information about the work order. And if you want to update some of the details, you can click **Provide Work Order Update**.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-52.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
Next, you'll be presented with a variety of options for the details you'd like to update.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-53.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
Here's the list of the things you can do to update your maintenance request. You may click each links for the separate help guides.

* **[Reschedule the Job/Update Your Availability](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/How-can-a-Resident-Update-Maintenance-Schedule-Availability)**: If your availability changes, you can adjust the time slots.
* **[Mark the Request as Completed](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/How-can-a-Resident-Confirm-Completion-of-a-Work-Order)**: Once the maintenance is finished, you can indicate that the work order is done.
* **[Report an Incomplete Request](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/How-can-a-Resident-Report-an-Incomplete-Request)**: If the issue isn't resolved, you can select this option.
* **[Add Images and Files](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/How-can-a-Resident-Add-Images-and-Files-on-a-Work-Order)**: Attach images or files related to the work order.
* [**Rate Service Provided**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Rating-Completed-Maintenance-Request-Service): Share your experience and provide feedback on the service.
* **[I Need Help](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Requesting-Assistance-from-Latchel-Via-Work-Order)**: If you require further assistance related to the maintenance request.
* **[Generate PDF](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/How-can-a-Resident-Generate-Work-Order-Details-in-PDF)**: Save or print a copy of the work order for your records.
