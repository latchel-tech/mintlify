### **5 easy steps on how to use the drain snake.**

1\. Open the sink drain's stopper.

* Chances are, you won't even have to remove the stopper. Just lift it up with your hands or open it using the stopper lever just above or below your bathroom faucet. Unclog a bathroom sink - lift drain stopper.

2\. Place hair snake into bathroom sink drain.

* Place the sharp end of the hair snake into the drain with pointy thorns facing up. how to use drain snake to unclog a bathroom sink - place plastic drain snake into the drain.

3\. Vigorously move around hair snake.

* We suggest you put on some rubber gloves for this step. After the snake is well into the drain, begin to pull it back up, then down, and side to side. You can rotate it, spin it, whatever. The idea here is to get the thorns (the pointy sides) of the snake to snag any hair (or other clingy debris) in the drain. unclog a bathroom sink - move the snake up, and down.

4\. Pull out hair snake and clean.

* Make sure you have a trash bag nearby. You'll want to clean hair and debris from the drain snake and place it into a trash bag.

5\. Run water and test drain flow.

* In conclusion, run your water for a while. Does it drain quickly now? If so, you're done. If not, repeat **step 3** and try again.

### **How to Request for a Drain Pack**

Access your **Resident Benefit Hub**. Contact the Latchel Maintenance team to request for your online hub link. To know more about this follow, [Accessing Your Resident Benefits Hub](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Accessing-Your-Resident-Benefits-Hub).

Click **My Benefits** from your Latchel online portal.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-7.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Once in the Resident Benefit page, click **Latchel Benefits** to sort all the resident benefits showing. Look for the "**Free Maintenance Drain Pack"** benefit then click **Learn More.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-8.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Next, you will be taken to the **Free Drain Pack Request form**. Click **Start** to fill out the form. Make sure you are typing your correct information especially your property address.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-9.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

At the end of the form, click **Submit**. Expect a follow-up within 5-7 business days.
