#### Frequently Asked Questions About Cash Reimbursements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much cash reimbursement a resident can file?">
    Under the cash reimbursements scheme, residents have the opportunity to claim up to $1,000 for damages that would ordinarily fall under their responsibility.

  <Accordion title="How do I submit for cash reimbursements?">
    \*\* Follow these **four easy steps.**

    1. Access your Resident Benefit Hub. Contact Latchel maintenance team and you will be provided with a link to your hub. To know more about this follow, [Accessing Your Resident Benefits Hub](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Accessing-Your-Resident-Benefits-Hub)**.**

    2. From your **My Benefits** page, click **Latchel Benefits**, and for this example look for **Lockout Reimbursement**. Then, click **Learn More**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-2.jpg" alt="image.png" />
    </Frame>

     

    1. Next, you will be taken to the **Resident Reimbursement Request Form**. Click **Start** to fill out the form. Submit a before and after photo of the damage along with the invoice.

    <Note>
      Remember to choose **"Lockout"** on question #6 within the form.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-3.jpg" alt="image.png" />
    </Frame>

    1. At the end of the form, click **Submit**. Expect a follow-up with 5-7 business days.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
