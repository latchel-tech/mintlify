Access your **property management's resident portal**.

From your resident dashboard, a **banner** similar to the image below must be displayed. Click the **Latchel link** to submit your maintenance request.

<Frame>
  ![Maintenancebanner](/images/maintenancebanner.png)
</Frame>

In the next page that opens on your browser, enter your **full name**, **phone number**, and **email address**. Then click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 01 09 2026 07 31 AM](/images/Latchel-01-09-2026_07_31_AM.png)
</Frame>

Confirm the **property address** by clicking **Select**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 01 09 2026 07 34 AM](/images/Latchel-01-09-2026_07_34_AM.png)
</Frame>

On each subsequent page, please **answer the questions related to your concern** and **provide your availability**. Then, click the **Next Question** button to proceed to the following steps. Once all the necessary information is provided, click **Submit Service Request**.

<Note>
  Latchel will reach back out to you once an appointment slot is confirmed with a vendor.
</Note>
