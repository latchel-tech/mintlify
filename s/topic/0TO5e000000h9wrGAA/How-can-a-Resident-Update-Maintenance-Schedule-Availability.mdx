---
title: "Updating Availability (Resident)"
description: "As a resident, you have access to your Resident Dashboard from where you can access your existing and past maintenance requests. If you need to update your availability, follow the steps below."
---

First, navigate to your dashboard by following the steps in the help guide: [**Resident Dashboard Overview**](https://help.latchel.com/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Resident-Dashboard-Overview). Once logged in, select the **work order** you need to update your availability.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-38.jpg)
</Frame>

On the **Work Order Details** page, click **Provide Work Order Update**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-39.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Then select **Update Availability**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-40.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

The current slot availability will now be displayed. If you wish to change the current schedule, simply click the **Update** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-41.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

From here **choose a new schedule** or **customize the time slot** that is convenient for you.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-42.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Finally, click **Submit Availability**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-43.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

And that's it! You've successfully updated your availability in your maintenance request.
