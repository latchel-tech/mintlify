---
title: "Requesting Assistance from Latchel Via Work Order"
description: "As a resident, you have access to your **Resident Dashboard** from where you can access your existing and past maintenance requests. If you **need assistance** in any of your existing or recent maintenance request, you may follow the steps below."
---

First, navigate to your **resident dashboard** by following the steps in the help guide:

<Card title="Resident Dashboard Overview" icon="chalkboard-user" iconType="duotone" href="/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Resident-Dashboard-Overview">
  Learn how to access and navigate your Resident Dashboard for the first time with ease.
</Card>

From your dashboard, **select the work order** which you need an assistance.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-65.jpg)
</Frame>

On the Work Order Details page, click **Provide Work Order Update** button.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-66.jpg)
</Frame>

Then select **I Need Help**.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-67.jpg)
</Frame>

Now identify if your concern is either **Time Sensitive** or **Not Time Sensitive**.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-68.jpg)
</Frame>

If it's a **time-sensitive concern**, we advise you to call Latchel directly by dialing the number that is shown under **Call our team at**Â to resolve the issue immediately and provide the **Reference Work Order Number** to the Latchel Associate. Then, click **Done** to exit from the page.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-69.jpg)
</Frame>

If it's **not a time-sensitive concern**, provide the details of your concern and click **Send**. Based on our volume of assistance requests, a Latchel Associate will reach out to you during business hours.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-70.jpg)
</Frame>

And that's it\! Your General Help will be received by Latchel. One of our dedicated team members will review your message shortly and respond to you with the utmost care.
