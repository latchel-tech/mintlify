description: "First, navigate to your **[maintenance dashboard](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wqGAA/How-to-View-the-Resident-Maintenance-Requests-Dashboard)**. From here, select which work order you need additional assistance."
mode: wide

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-65.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

On the Work Order Details page, click **Provide Work Order Update** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-66.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Then select **I Need Help**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-67.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Now identify if your concern is either **Time Sensitive** or **Not Time Sensitive**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-68.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

If it's a **time-sensitive concern**, we advise you to call Latchel directly by dialing the number that is shown under **Call our team at** to resolve the issue immediately and provide the **Reference Work Order Number** to the Latchel Associate. Then, click **Done** to exit from the page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-69.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

If it's **not a time-sensitive concern**, provide the details of your concern and click **Send**. Based on our volume of assistance requests, a Latchel Associate will reach out to you during business hours.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-70.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

And that's it! Your General Help will be received by Latchel. One of our dedicated team members will review your message shortly and respond to you with the utmost care.
