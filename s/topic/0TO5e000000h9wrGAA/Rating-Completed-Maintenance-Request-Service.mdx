---
title: "Rating the Service Provided (Resident)"
description: "Latchel values customer feedback! Here are the steps you can follow to leave a review about your recent experience with a maintenance request."
---

For each completed maintenance request, you'll receive a notification to review your experience. In the email, click **Leave a Review** to be directed to the work order detail page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-31.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

On the work order details page, click **Provide Work Order Update** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-32.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

Then select **Rate Service Provided**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-33.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

From here, rate your experience, leave any additional comments based on the work of the service provider, and then hit **Submit**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-34.jpg" alt="image.png" />
</Frame>

And that's all there is to it! Your property manager and Latchel greatly appreciate the ratings you've provided.
