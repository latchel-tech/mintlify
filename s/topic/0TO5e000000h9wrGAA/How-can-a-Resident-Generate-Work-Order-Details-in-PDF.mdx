---
title: "Generating Work Order Details (Resident)"
description: "As a resident, you have access to your **Resident Dashboard** from where you can access your existing and past maintenance requests. If you need to **generate work order details** of your existing or past maintenance request, follow these steps below."
---

First, navigate to your **resident dashboard** by following the steps in the help guide:

<Card title="Resident Dashboard Overview" icon="chalkboard-user" iconType="duotone" href="/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Resident-Dashboard-Overview">
  Learn how to access and navigate your Resident Dashboard for the first time with ease.
</Card>

From your dashboard, **select the work order** you’d like to generate details for.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-35.jpg)
</Frame>

Then, on the Work Order Details page, click **Provide Work Order Update**.

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-36.jpg)
</Frame>

Lastly, select **Generate PDF**. Depending on your computer settings, it will either download automatically or ask you to save it on your computer. 

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-1-37.jpg)
</Frame>

And there you have it\! You have successfully generated your work order details in pdf\!
