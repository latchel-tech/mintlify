Then, on the Work Order Details page, click **Provide Work Order Update**.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-36.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
Lastly, select **Generate PDF. **Depending on your computer settings, it will either download automatically or ask you to save it on your computer. 
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-1-37.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
And there you have it! You have successfully generated your work order details in pdf!
