---
title: "How to Reject a Work Order (Service Provider)"
description: "This help guide explains the process of responding to new work orders once received. When a work order becomes available, you'll receive an SMS message and email notification with a link to the work order details. From there, you can choose **to accept** or **decline** the work order."
mode: "wide"
---

First, **login to your Latchel dashboard** by following the steps in:

<Card title="Accessing Your Latchel Portal (Service Providers)" icon="chalkboard-user" iconType="duotone" color="#ff9b8a" href="/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/Accessing-your-Latchel-portal">
  Learn how to log into your Latchel portal and access your account.
</Card>

If you want to reject a work order, go to your dashboard you will see a **number of new work orders available**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage.png)
</Frame>

**Select** the work order you want to reject.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-2.png)
</Frame>

Click the **ellipsis** at the top right of the work order page.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-3.png)
</Frame>

From the list of option on the page, click the **Remove Me From This Work Order**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-4.png)
</Frame>

Then select **Yes, remove me now**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-5.png)
</Frame>

<br />

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-6.png)
</Frame>

And that's how you can reject a work order\!
