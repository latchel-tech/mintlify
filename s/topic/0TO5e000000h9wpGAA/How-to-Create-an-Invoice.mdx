### **Steps to Create an Invoice**

From your work order page click the **Provider Work Order Update** button located at the top right side of the page. Then select **Add Invoice**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-98.jpg)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-99.jpg)
</Frame>

You could either **Upload Invoice** to upload the image of the file or **Create an Invoice**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-100.jpg)
</Frame>

If you click **Create Invoice**, you can provide payment instructions. In case you encounter damage caused by the resident, you may toggle the switch just across the question about resident-caused damage.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-101.jpg)
</Frame>

Next, click the **\+Add Item** to specify the line item in your invoice and click **Next**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-103.jpg)
</Frame>

Then describe the work performed. Upload the **Before** and **After** images of the job, and hit **Next**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-104.jpg)
</Frame>

Now you can see the generated invoice overview and check if the details are all correct, then click **Submit Invoice**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-105.jpg)
</Frame>

Provide the work order status if **Yes, it's done** (to mark the work order as complete) or **There's More To Do** (provide information on what is needed to complete).Â 

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-106.jpg)
</Frame>

And that's it! You have successfully created an invoice for your completed work order! You may also check the video below for a clearer vision of the steps!
