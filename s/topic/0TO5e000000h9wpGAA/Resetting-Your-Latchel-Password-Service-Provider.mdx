### **Steps to Reset Password**

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Need resetting your password? Click here!" icon="shield-keyhole" color="#ff9b8a" horizontal href="https://app.latchel.com/login">
    
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Click the button above. Then, click **Forgot Password**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/pw1.png)
</Frame>

Enter the **email address** associated with your Latchel account, then click **Reset Password**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/pw2.png)
</Frame>

A confirmation message will appear, indicating that an email with reset instructions has been sent.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/pw3.png)
</Frame>

Check your inbox for an email from Latchel containing a password reset link. Open the email and click Reset Password.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/pw4.png)
</Frame>

In the new tab that opens, **enter your new password**, then **re-enter it to confirm**. Click **Submit Password** to complete the reset process. You will then be taken to your

<Frame>
  ![](/images/pw5.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Your new password must include at least **one uppercase letter**, **one lowercase letter**, and **a special character**.
</Tip>

Once completed, you will be redirected to your **Work Orders** page in Latchel.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/pw6.png)
</Frame>

And that's how easy you can reset your password\!
