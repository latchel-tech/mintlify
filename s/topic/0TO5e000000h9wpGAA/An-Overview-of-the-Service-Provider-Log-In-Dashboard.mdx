As a Service Provider, when you access your dashboard, you'll be taken to the public dashboard where you can see four columns showing the **Action Required**, **Awaiting Work Date**, **In Progress**, and **Done** status.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-12.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Sign In to Latchel**

However, Latchel wants to ensure you have an even better experience. To view your full dashboard, click **SIGN IN**, which you will find in the blue bar at the top center of the dashboard. 

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-13.jpg)
</Frame>

And even if the blue ban was closed for any reason, you can still see a note on the top where you can click **Log In**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-14.jpg)
</Frame>

**First time logging in? Check the guide below for more details on how you can access your Latchel dashboard\!**

<Card title="Accessing Your Latchel Portal (Service Providers)" icon="chalkboard-user" iconType="duotone" color="#ff9b8a" href="/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/Accessing-your-Latchel-portal">
  Learn how to log into your Latchel portal and access your account.

Once you click the **Log In** option, enter your **username** (your email address associated with your service provider user) and **password**, then click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-15.jpg)
</Frame>

Once inside, you’ll have access to many more columns and detailed information about your work orders, including those waiting for approval. All of your work orders will be categorized based on the following provided columns: **Scheduling**, **Confirming Completion**, **Awaiting Invoice**, **Scheduled Today**, **Scheduled Later**, **Other Work Orders**, **Completed**, and **Cancelled**.

In addition, you'll also have access to your **Invoices**, **Account Settings**, and the option which will take you to the **Latchel Help Center**. Let's delve to each of these features\!

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-16.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **The Maintenance Dashboard**

As soon as you log in, you will be taken to your **Maintenance Dashboard**. As mentioned, you will have access to **multiple columns** corresponding to the status of your work orders. Also, notice **the number of work orders assigned to you**, which you will see at the top of the page.

  ![](/images/rtaImage-17.jpg)
</Frame>

<Frame>

From this page, you will have access to all of your work orders -- from open work orders that needs your immediate attention to the previously completed and canceled work orders.

  For work orders that require updates, please refer to the help guide [**A Service Provider’s Guide to Updating a Work Order**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/A-Service-Provider-s-Guide-in-Updating-a-Work-Order) for detailed instructions on the available options.
</Tip>

#### **The Account Settings**

If you need to modify your account settings, just go to the top corner of your dashboard and click on **Account Settings.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-25.jpg)
</Frame>

<Check>
  Here are some of the items you can modify inside the **Account Settings**:

  - **Details:** This tab will show your company address. _If you wish to update your company/office address, please contact Latchel._
  - **Integration:** In this tab, you can set up your Google Calendar integration. To know more about this, please refer to [**Google Calendar Integration (Service Provider).**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/Google-Calendar-Integration-Service-Provider)
  - **Payment Settings**: This tab will let you add your preferred payment method. You can follow the steps shown in the guide [**Adding Bank Account Information (Service Provider).**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/How-can-a-Service-Provider-Add-Their-Bank-Account-Information)
  - **Users**: This tab will take you to the list of different service provider users within your company. If you are the admin and need to update your company users, please check the help guide [**Managing Service Provider Users**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/Managing-Service-Provider-Users) for more details.
</Check>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-26.jpg)

#### **The Latchel Help Center**

Lastly, by clicking your **profile icon** at the upper right corner, you will have the option to go to our **Help Center** page.

Accessing the **Help Center** enables you to quickly resolve issues independently, enhances efficiency, and promotes self-service, while keeping you informed about Latchel's features and updates that can improve your service delivery and work order management.
