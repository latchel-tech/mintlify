### **Accepting and Scheduling Work Order**

If you want to view this work order, in your dashboard you will see a **1 new job available** notification.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-80.jpg)
</Frame>

When you click the notification it will direct you to the page, where all of the available work orders for you to accept and schedule.

Click **View Work Orde**r, to view the details and other information of the work order.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-81.jpg)
</Frame>

Now, after reviewing all the details, hit the **Accept & Schedule Job** button to proceed.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-82.jpg)
</Frame>

Then choose which **user** will be assigned to the work order. In this instance let's select the **primary user**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-83.jpg)
</Frame>

From here you can accept and set a schedule based on the resident's availability.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-84.jpg)
</Frame>

<br />

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-85.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Aside from the available schedule window you could also select I spoke to the Resident (if you and the resident have another schedule agreement.)
</Note>

After selecting the schedule, click **Submit Schedule**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-86.jpg)
</Frame>

All set, the work order will move to the Awaiting Work Date status!
