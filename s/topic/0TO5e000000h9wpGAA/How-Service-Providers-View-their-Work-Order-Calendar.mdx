### **Checking your Calendar**

Sign in to your Latchel service provider email address. From the Service Provider page, click the **initials icon** located at the top right corner, then select **Account Settings** on the dropdown.

<Frame>
  ![Serviceprovideraccountset Pn](/images/serviceprovideraccountset.png)
</Frame>

In the Account Settings page click the **Users** tab. Select which **Service Provider User** you want to view the work order calendar.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-141.jpg)
</Frame>

On User's page click the **Calendar** tab, this will display the work order schedule of the specific user.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-142.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Each work order in the calendar can be clicked and expanded to show the details of the work order and could also direct you to the work order page view.
</Note>

#### **How to Print the Calendar View**

On the calendar page, click **Print View** which will give you a larger view of the calendar.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-143.jpg)
</Frame>

Located on the top right corner, click the **Print** icon which enables you to print all of the scheduled work orders for the week.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-144.jpg)
</Frame>

<Check>
  The calendar will always be in the time zone of the person who is viewing the calendar.
</Check>
