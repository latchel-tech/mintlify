---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-140.jpg"/>
</Frame>

In the Account Settings page click the **Users** tab. Select which **Service Provider User** you want to view the work order calendar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-141.jpg"/>
</Frame>


On User's page click the **Calendar** tab, this will display the work order schedule of the specific user.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-142.jpg"/>
</Frame>


<Info>
  **NOTE:** _Each work order in the calendar can be clicked and expanded to show the details of the work order and could also direct you to the work order page view._
</Info>

#### **How to Print the Calendar View**

On the calendar page, click **Print View** which will give you a larger view of the calendar.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-143.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Located on the top right corner, click the **Print** icon which enables you to print all of the scheduled work orders for the week.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-144.jpg"/>
</Frame>

<Info>

**NOTE:** _The calendar will always be in the time zone of the person who is viewing the calendar._

</Info>
