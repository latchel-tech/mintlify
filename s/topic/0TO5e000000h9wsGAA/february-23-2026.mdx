2. **New Service: Non-Maintenance Emergencies (Latchel On-Call)**

You can now use Latchel for more than just maintenance. With **Latchel On-Call**, we receive and route urgent non-maintenance issues to your team — helping you respond faster to situations like:

- Noise complaints
- Smoking violations
- Security concerns
- On-site management or safety issues

<Icon icon="lightbulb-on" iconType="duotone" color="#ff9b8a" size={25} /> **NEW: Add Photos & Videos After an On-Call Alert is Created**

3. **Editable Task Titles**

We’ve added the ability for PM users to edit task titles after creation. We heard feedback that task details often evolve as situations change, and updating titles helps keep tasks clear and accurate. Now, when editing a task, you can update the title along with the other editable fields, and the new title will appear everywhere the task is displayed!

### **Resolved Bugs**

**Duplicate Inspection Areas**

We fixed an issue where inspection areas could appear multiple times for property managers with more than one inspection template. Inspection areas will now display correctly without duplicates, ensuring a cleaner and more consistent inspection setup.
