---
title: How to Add a Service Provider to a Portfolio
description: "When you want to assign a service provider to a portfolio that is not currently in the same territory or zip code as the portfolio, you can follow the steps to assign a service provider."
---

From your dashboard, click **Places** and then **Portfolio**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-9.jpg" alt="pasted image 0 (3).png"/>
</Frame>


From the Portfolio page, you can **click** on the Portfolio you'd like to configure or **search** it on the search bar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-10.jpg" alt="pasted image 0 (3).png"/>
</Frame>


On the portfolio page, click on the **Service Providers** tab and select **Categories & Priorities**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-11.jpg" alt="pasted image 0 (3).png"/>
</Frame>

From the skill/categories, click on the **dropdown icon** and then **\+ Include Service Provider.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-14.jpg" alt="pasted image 0 (3).png"/>
</Frame>

Then list of Service Providers available will appear and you may add them by clicking **\+ include** beside their names. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-12.jpg" alt="pasted image 0 (3).png"/>
</Frame>


On the pop-up window, **select** the property(s) you want the service provided to be added.

To finalize the configuration/change, click on **Continue**. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-13.jpg" alt="pasted image 0 (3).png"/>
</Frame>
 

Do you also need to update your service provider's skills? Check this help guide: **[How to Prioritize/Edit Service Providers With Their Skills](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wqGAA/How-to-Prioritize-Edit-Service-Providers-with-their-skills)**
