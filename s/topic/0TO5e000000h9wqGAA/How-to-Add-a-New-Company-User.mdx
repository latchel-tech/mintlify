title: How to Add and Remove Company User
description: "This help guide will walk you through the simple steps on how you can add and remove/disable company users in your Latchel Account Settings. Let's get started!"
mode: wide
---

#### **Add New Company User**

#### After you logged in to Latchel, click the **arrow down** beside your user icon at the top right corner of your dashboard. Then, click **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-208.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From the **Account Settings** page, go to the **Users tab.** Then, click the **+New User** button at the top right.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-209.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From the small window that shows on the page, **enter the new company user's information**. Click **Save** once done.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-210.jpg"/>
</Frame>

<Info>
  **NOTE:** _The name of the new user is required, while the phone number and email address are optional. However, providing the new user's contact information is highly recommended for seamless communication._
</Info>

#### **Remove Existing Company User**

#### From you **Account Settings**, go to **Users** tab.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-211.jpg"/>
</Frame>

In the list of the Company Users, **look for the name you need to remove/disable**. Then, click the **3 dots** across the name of the user to be removed/disabled.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-212.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Next, choose **Disable User**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-213.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Finally, click **Submit** to confirm the action you've taken. You'll notice a note **User Account Disabled** under the user's name upon submission.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-214.jpg"/>
</Frame>

And that's how you can add and remove/disable a company user!
