#### **Work Order-Related Concerns**

From the top right corner of your dashboard, click the **SMS icon**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-570.jpg"/>
</Frame>


In the message section under the **"My Messages"** tab click **Latchel Support**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-571.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Then place your concern on the pop-up message that will show in the lower right corner. Hit **Enter** on your keyboard to send the message.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-572.jpg"/>
</Frame>



You can also select the **New Message icon** to search and tag your concern to a specific work order.

To learn how to tag SMS, you may visit **[How to Tag SMS Conversations Started by a Property Manager](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wqGAA/How-to-Tag-SMS-Conversations-Started-by-a-Property-Manager).**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-573.jpg"/>
</Frame>


#### **Non-Work Order-Related Concerns**

For any non-work order-related concerns or any account-related inquiries, please contact our Customer Success team by emailing **success@latchel.com.**
