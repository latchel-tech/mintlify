### **Your Customer Success Manager**

For **training sessions**, **major configuration updates**, **company structure changes**, or **meetings that need coordination or follow-ups**, your **CSM** is happy to help! These meetings are scheduled based on your CSM’s calendar availability.

### **Email Latchel**

For **technical support**, **configuration assistance**, **account settings**, or **billing-related questions**, please reach out to [success@latchel.com](mailto:success@latchel.com) — our team will get back to you within **1 business day**.

### **Work Order-Related Concerns**

For active **work order updates**, including **emergency work orders**, simply text **(not call)** your assigned maintenance hotline number or use webchat. Our team will respond **within 10 minutes**.

- **The Latchel Webchat**

From the top right corner of your dashboard, click the **SMS icon**.

Then place your concern on the pop-up message that will show in the lower right corner. Hit **Enter** on your keyboard to send the message.
