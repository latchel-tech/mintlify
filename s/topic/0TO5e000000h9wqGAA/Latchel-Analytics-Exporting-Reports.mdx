### **Export Key Metrics**

Within each key metric tile, hidden options appear in the corner.

<Frame>
  ![Image 02 25 2026 09 04 AM](/images/image-02-25-2026_09_04_AM.png)
</Frame>

Click the **ellipsis** (<Icon icon="ellipsis-vertical" />) then select **Export chart**. You can export the report by:

1. **Export Data:** This option allows you to save the file as a **CSV, CSV (Excel), or Google Sheets** file.
2. **Save as PNG:** Save your chart as an **image** on your computer.
3. **Copy Image to Clipboard:** If you do not want to save the image file, you can copy it to your clipboard and **paste it into the document** you are working on.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 02 25 2026 09 07 AM](/images/Latchel-02-25-2026_09_07_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Export Data (CSV, CSV for Excel, Google Sheets)**

<Tip>
  **Key Benefits:**

  - Allows deeper analysis using spreadsheet tools (formulas, pivot tables, custom charts).
  - Makes it easy to share raw data with owners, leadership, or accounting teams.
  - Supports record-keeping and documentation outside of Latchel.
  - Enables combining Latchel data with other operational or financial datasets.
  - Useful for audits, reporting meetings, and portfolio reviews.
</Tip>

Click the **three dots** on the report you wish to export.

<Frame>
  ![Image 02 25 2026 09 04 AM](/images/image-02-25-2026_09_04_AM.png)
</Frame>

Then click **Export chart**. From the options that show up, click **Export data**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 03 02 2026 07 29 AM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-03-02-2026_07_29_AM.png)
</Frame>

Choose your preferred **file type**, then click **Export** to download the report.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 03 02 2026 07 47 AM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-03-02-2026_07_47_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  To export the file as **Google Sheets**, you will need to sign in to your Gmail account the first time.
</Warning>

### **Save as PNG**

<Tip>
  **Key Benefits:**

  - Quickly download a clean visual of your chart for presentations or reports.
  - Ideal for owner reports, performance decks, or internal summaries.
  - Preserves the exact visual format of your dashboard view.
  - No need to recreate charts manually in another tool.
</Tip>

Click the **three dots** on the report you wish to export.

<Frame>
  ![Image 02 25 2026 09 04 AM](/images/image-02-25-2026_09_04_AM.png)
</Frame>

Then click **Export chart**. From the options that show up, click **Save as PNG**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 03 02 2026 07 54 AM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-03-02-2026_07_54_AM.png)
</Frame>

Depending on your browser settings, the image may automatically download to your computer or prompt you to choose a location—click **Save** if prompted.

### **Copy Image to Clipboard**

<Tip>
  **Key Benefits:**

  - Fastest way to paste charts directly into emails, Slack, or documents.
  - Eliminates the need to download and manage image files.
  - Great for quick updates, team discussions, or executive summaries.
</Tip>

Click the **three dots** on the report you wish to export.

<Frame>
  ![Image 02 25 2026 09 04 AM](/images/image-02-25-2026_09_04_AM.png)
</Frame>

Then click **Export chart**. From the options that show up, click **Copy image to clipboard**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 03 02 2026 07 58 AM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-03-02-2026_07_58_AM.png)
</Frame>

After copying, you can paste the image directly into any document where it’s needed.
