---
title: "How to Delete a Portfolio"
description: "Through this Help Guide, property managers will learn how to delete portfolios that are no longer needed."
mode: "wide"
---

On your dashboard click **Places** and then select **Portfolios** on the dropdown.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-675.jpg)
</Frame>

Then search for the Portfolio name in the **Search bar**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-676.jpg)
</Frame>

Once you found the portfolio name, navigate to the right side of the page, click the **ellipses icon,** and select **Delete Portfolio**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-677.jpg)
</Frame>

A pop-up message will appear, and to confirm action click **OK**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-678.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Deleting a portfolio will not affect the status of the properties included in it. The purpose of deleting a portfolio is to ungroup the properties.
</Note>
