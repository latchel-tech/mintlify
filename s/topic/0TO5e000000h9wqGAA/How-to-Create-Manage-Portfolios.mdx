### **How to Create a Portfolio**

On the upper left-hand side of your dashboard click Places then select Portfolios.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-375.jpg)
</Frame>

On the right-hand side of your portal, click **New Portfolio.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-376.jpg)
</Frame>

From here, you can create a name for your portfolio and choose to select the assigned Property Manager from the members added to your account. Click **Create.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-377.jpg)
</Frame>

To add properties to your portfolio, click the **Properties** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-378.jpg)
</Frame>

Click **Add Property** on the left-hand side of your portal.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-379.jpg)
</Frame>

Search for the property address and click on the address to add the property to your portfolio.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-380.jpg)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-381.jpg)
</Frame>

### **How to Manage a Portfolio**

Overseeing a portfolio is similar in many ways to managing at the company level, as many of the settings and functionalities are quite alike.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-382.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **A. The Details Tab**

Company users may modify the following information in this tab such as:

- Assigned property manager.
- Assigned owner. 
- Emergency Escalation Contacts
- Portal Settings
- Work Order Budget Limits
- Emergency Temperature Settings
- Non-Emergency Scheduling

#### **Assigning/Updating the Assign PM User**

From the **Details** tab, under the **Property Manager section**, click on the **three dots or ellipse** and click on **Reassign PM User**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-383.jpg)
</Frame>

Click the **arrow** to show the list of Property Managers and select the name of the new Property Manager. 

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-384.jpg)
</Frame>

A confirmation box will appear to review the Property Manager information, and click on **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-385.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **B. The Subscription Tab**

This section provides your subscription information including status, start date, and end date of subscriptions. 

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-386.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **C. The Maintenance Tab**

This tab allows you to view and search for work orders that are under the portfolio. 

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-387.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **D. The Properties Tab**

This tab displays all the active properties linked to the specified portfolio. Users have the convenience of searching for an address by typing it into the **search bar**. Additionally, new properties can be added by simply clicking the **\+Add Property** button.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-388.jpg)
</Frame>

From this tab, you can also **rename your portfolio**. Simply click the **existing name** at the top of the page, **type in the new name**, and then click **Save** to save the changes you made.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-389.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **E. The Service Providers Tab**

This tab allows any company users to manage the preferred service providers at the portfolio level. Service provider prioritization can be modified here including Latchel Contrated Network Service Provider.
