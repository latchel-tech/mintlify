### **How to Download Properties and Residents' Report**

Sign in to your property management software. Make sure you are also signed in to the Latchel Data Sync Extension.

<Frame>
  ![1screenshot 2025 05 02 231523 05 02 2025 11 16 PM Pn](/images/1Screenshot-2025-05-02-231523-05-02-2025_11_16_PM.png)
</Frame>

Once logged in, click the **Latchel Data Sync icon** located on the right side of the page.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 04 29105555(1) Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-29105555\(1\).png)
</Frame>

Click the **three dots** in the top-right corner of the extension and select:

- **Download tenants** to export a tenant report, or
- **Download properties** to export a property report.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02232120(1) Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02232120\(1\).png)
</Frame>

You’ll see a message saying **“Preparing your property (or tenant) report.”**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02231040 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02231040.png)
</Frame>

Once the message **“Export Complete”** appears, you will be prompted to save the file to your computer.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02231058 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02231058.png)
</Frame>

### **How to Report a Problem**

If you’re experiencing issues with your Latchel integration or the extension, you can report the problem by selecting **Report a problem**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02232120(2) Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02232120\(2\).png)
</Frame>

Enter a brief description of the issue in the box that appears, then click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![2screenshot 2025 05 02 232451 05 02 2025 11 37 PM Pn](/images/2Screenshot-2025-05-02-232451-05-02-2025_11_37_PM.png)
</Frame>

A confirmation message will appear on your screen, letting you know that Latchel has received your feedback.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02232527 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02232527.png)
</Frame>

### **How to Give a Feedback**

Latchel values your feedback and uses it to improve your experience. You can quickly share suggestions or report issues directly through the Latchel Data Sync Extension. Here's how.

Click the three dots in the top-right corner of the extension and select Give feedback.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02232120(3) Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02232120\(3\).png)
</Frame>

A new browser tab will open with a Typeform.

<Frame>
  ![3screenshot 2025 05 02 234802 05 02 2025 11 48 PM Pn](/images/3Screenshot-2025-05-02-234802-05-02-2025_11_48_PM.png)
</Frame>

Fill out the form with as much detail as possible. You can also attach a screenshot or recording to better describe your feedback. When you're finished, click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![4screenshot 2025 05 02 234602 05 02 2025 11 50 PM Pn](/images/4Screenshot-2025-05-02-234602-05-02-2025_11_50_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **How to Manually Sync Properties and Residents**

**Auto-sync is now always on by default** with the new Latchel Data Sync Extension, **refreshing data every 4 hours**. However, if you need to manually sync your data, here’s how you can do it.

In the Latchel Data Sync Extension, click the link in the message: **“Latchel syncs your data every four hours. To perform a manual sync, please click here.”**

<Frame>
  ![Image 07 23 2025 01 01 PM Pn](/images/image-07-23-2025_01_01_PM.png)
</Frame>

 You will be given **two options** to choose from:

- Sync Latchel Work Orders here (Faster)
- Sync data in both direction (Slower)

Then, click **Start Sync**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2025 07 23 125005 07 23 2025 12 55 PM Pn](/images/Screenshot-2025-07-23-125005-07-23-2025_12_55_PM.png)
</Frame>

Your screen may be busy while the sync is in progress.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02235717 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02235717.png)
</Frame>

Once complete, you’ll see a message that says: **“Your data has been synced with Latchel.”**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 02235753 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-02235753.png)
</Frame>

You’ll notice that the last sync time is now updated in the extension.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2025 05 03000252(1) Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-05-03000252\(1\).png)
</Frame>

That’s it\! That’s how simple—and awesome—the new version of the Latchel Data Sync Extension is. We hope you enjoy the improved experience\!
