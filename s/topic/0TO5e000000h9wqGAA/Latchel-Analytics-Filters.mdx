title: "Latchel Analytics: Using the Filters"

---

Filters allow you to narrow down your analytics results to focus only on the information that matters most. In Latchel Analytics, you can filter data in three ways: **Date Range**, **Portfolio**, and **Owner**.

<Tip>
  You can combine filters (e.g., Date Range \+ Portfolio \+ Owner) for more precise results.
</Tip>

### **Filtering by Date Range**

Go to the **Date Range** filter in your Analytics view.

Select one of the available options:

- **Last**
- **Between**
- **On**
- **Before**
- **After**
- **Next**
- **Current**
- **Custom Date:** This option will ask you to enter a start and end date, either as a Fixed date or a Relative date as shown below.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 11 59 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_11_59_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Frame caption="Custom Date Range">
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 11 58 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_11_58_AM.png)
</Frame>

Enter the **number of units** (e.g., “7” for 7 days).

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 12 00 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_12_00_PM.png)
</Frame>

Choose your **preferred unit of time**:

- **Days**
- **Weeks (starting Sunday or Monday)**
- **Months**
- **Quarters**
- **Hours**
- **Minutes**

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 11 47 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_11_47_AM.png)
</Frame>

There is also an option to **include today's day** for you to select\!

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 11 48 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_11_48_AM.png)
</Frame>

The data will refresh to reflect your selected timeframe.

### **Filtering by Portfolio**

Click the **Portfolio filter**. Then, **select one or more portfolios** from the list.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 12 13 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_12_13_PM.png)
</Frame>

Results will automatically adjust to show data only for the chosen portfolio(s).

### **Filtering by Owner**

Click the **Owner filter**. Choose the **owner’s name** from the list.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 08 27 2025 12 22 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-08-27-2025_12_22_PM.png)
</Frame>

Analytics will update to display results specific to that owner.
