---
title: "How to Terminate the Gmail Integration"
description: "With Latchel, you have the option to integrate your Google Gmail account, enabling you to effortlessly send automated notifications for new work orders and non-emergency budgets and/or work order approvals directly to your owners."
mode: "wide"
---

To terminate this integration, follow the steps below.

When logged into Latchel, click your initials/avatar on the top-right of the page, and on the dropdown click **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  ![1latchel Pn](/images/1Latchel.png)
</Frame>

On the **Integrations** tab, click **Terminate Integration** under the Gmail section.

  ![Latchel 06 30 2025 12 15 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-06-30-2025_12_15_PM.png)
</Frame>
