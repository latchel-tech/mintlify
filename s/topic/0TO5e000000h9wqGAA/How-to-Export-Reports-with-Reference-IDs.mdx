### **Appfolio**

If you are using the **Appfolio along with Latchel Chrome Plugin Extension,** follow these steps on how you can export your property and tenant directory from Appfolio.

1. First, **login** to your property management software. Go to **Reports**, scroll to **Tenant Reports**, then click **Tenant Directory**.

   <Tip>
     Alternatively, you can simply search **“Tenant Directory”** using the **search bar** at the top of the page.
   </Tip>
2. In the **Customize Report** window, set **Tenant Status** and **Tenant Type** to **All**, and make sure to **show Active tenants only**. Then, click **Update**.
3. On the right side of the page, **uncheck all options** first. Then **manually select** the following. Make sure that the **rows are not grouped** under any category or information.
   - Property Street Address 1
   - Property Street Address 2
   - Property City
   - Property State
   - Property Zip
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Phone Numbers
   - Emails
   - Property ID
   - Unit ID
   - Tenant ID
   - Status (optional)
4. Next, click **Action** at the upper-right corner of the page, then choose **Export as CSV** or **Export as Excel**. **Save** the file to your computer when prompted.
5. Finally, **attach the file to email and send** it to Latchel.

### **Propertyware**

Reference IDs for residents and properties must be downloaded separately. Please follow the steps below to export each one.

**A. Residents**

Go to **Reports**. If you have existing report folders for **Contacts**, expand **Contact Reports**. Otherwise you can click **New Report** to create report for **Contacts**. Then click **View Report**.

<Frame>
  ![1propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 12 08 2025 04 42 AM Pn](/images/1Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-12-08-2025_04_42_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![2propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 12 08 2025 04 43 AM Pn](/images/2Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-12-08-2025_04_43_AM.png)
</Frame>

Expand **Contact: Summary**. Make sure the **Entity ID** is selected. Also, check the boxes of the following:

- **Full Name**
- **Email**
- **Mobile #**
- **Address**
- **Address Cont.**
- **City**
- **State**
- **Zip**

<Tip>
  **Entity ID** are your tenant's reference IDs which Latchel uses to identify tenants within the integration.
</Tip>

  ![3propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 12 08 2025 04 43 AM Pn](/images/3Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-12-08-2025_04_43_AM.png)

**B. Properties**

Go to **Reports**. If you have existing **Property Reports** within your report folders, **expand it** then select **Units**. Otherwise, click **New Report** at the top to create new report for Units.

<Frame>
  ![1propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 12 08 2025 04 42 AM Pn](/images/1Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-12-08-2025_04_42_AM.png)

Expand **Building Summary**, and make sure to check **Building Entity ID** **_<u>only</u>_** within this section.

<Tip>
  The **Building Entity ID** serves as the property reference ID for integration with Latchel. The **Unit Entity ID** identifies the unit address for multi-unit properties.
</Tip>

- **Unit Entity ID**
- **Unit Address**
- **Unit Address Cont.**
- **Unit City**
- **Unit State**
- **Unit Zip**

### **Rent Manager**

Exporting property and resident reports from Rent Manager is a simple two-step process: first, set up the report type, then export the report using that setup.

**Creating the Report Type**

<Check>
  If this type of report with the necessary fields has not yet been created in your Rent Manager, follow these steps. If you have already created a Unit Export with reference IDs, you can skip this and directly download the Unit Export from your Report Writer.
</Check>

Log in to your Rent Manager dashboard. Navigate to **Reports**, expand **Report Writer**, and then select **Report Writer Manager**.

<Frame>
  ![1desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 06 35 AM Pn](/images/1Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_06_35_AM.png)
</Frame>

From the **dropdown list** under Report Writer Manager, select **Unit**.

<Frame>
  ![2desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 06 40 AM Pn](/images/2Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_06_40_AM.png)
</Frame>

In the new list on the left, click **Unit Export**, then select the **Edit** button at the top.

<Frame>
  ![3desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 06 48 AM Pn](/images/3Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_06_48_AM.png)
</Frame>

Remove all formulas displayed under Report Columns by clicking the icon <Icon icon="circle-xmark" iconType="solid" color="#e40a0a" /> on each row. These columns are unnecessary for the Latchel integration.

<Frame>
  ![4desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 06 54 AM Pn](/images/4Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_06_54_AM.png)
</Frame>

In the **Insertable Fields** section, expand **Units**, then expand Addresses. **Double click** the following to add the fields:

- **Street 1**
- **Street 2**
- **City**
- **State**
- **Postal Code**

Notice that each field adds to the Report Columns as you double click them.

<Frame>
  ![5desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 07 01 AM Pn](/images/5Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_07_01_AM.png)
</Frame>

In the same Unit fields, scroll down to locate the **Unit ID**, then **double-click it** as well.

<Frame>
  ![6desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 07 07 AM Pn](/images/6Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_07_07_AM.png)
</Frame>

Next, expand **Property**, then scroll to **Property ID**. **Double-click** it to add it to the report columns.

<Frame>
  ![7desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 07 10 AM Pn](/images/7Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_07_10_AM.png)
</Frame>

Now, **add the tenants** details. Expand **Tenants**, then **locate and double-click** the following fields to add them:

- **Full Name**
- **Number**
- **Email**
- **Contact ID**

<Tip>
  Expand **User Defined Fields** to find **Full Name**, **Contact ID**, and **Email**. The **Number** field can be found under **Phone Numbers**.

  The **Contact ID** serves as the **Reference User ID** that Latchel uses to identify residents in the integration.
</Tip>

<Frame>
  ![5desktop Screenshot 12 09 2025 06 58 AM Pn](/images/5Desktop-screenshot-12-09-2025_06_58_AM.png)
</Frame>

\*\*Rename \*\*the report, then click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![6desktop Screenshot 12 09 2025 07 00 AM Pn](/images/6Desktop-screenshot-12-09-2025_07_00_AM.png)
</Frame>

This does not export your report yet. Continue with the steps below to export the new report type you just created.

**Exporting the Reference ID Report**

In the Reports tab, expand **Report Writer \> Units**, then select the **report you created** for reference IDs.

<Frame>
  ![1desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 07 28 AM Pn](/images/1Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_07_28_AM.png)
</Frame>

In the Report Options window that appears, **check the top box** to include all properties. Also, select **Inactive Properties**, then click **View Report**.

<Frame>
  ![2desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 07 34 AM Pn](/images/2Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_07_34_AM.png)
</Frame>

Once the generated report appears on your screen, click either **Export to Excel** or **Export to CSV**.

<Frame>
  ![3desktop Screenshot 12 08 2025 07 41 AM Pn](/images/3Desktop-screenshot-12-08-2025_07_41_AM.png)
</Frame>

Finally, click **Export** then **save on your computer** once prompted.

<Frame>
  ![4desktop Screenshot 12 09 2025 06 17 AM Pn](/images/4Desktop-screenshot-12-09-2025_06_17_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  You can also send the report directly to Latchel by selecting **Send As Email**!
</Tip>
