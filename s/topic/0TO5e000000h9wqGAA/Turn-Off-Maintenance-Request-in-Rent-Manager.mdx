---
title: "Turn Off Maintenance Requests in Rent Manager"
description: "If Latchel is managing maintenance for all your units, you can prevent tenants from submitting service requests through your Rent Manager portal. Follow the steps below to disable this feature and ensure all maintenance requests are routed through Latchel."
---

### **Disabling Tenant to Create Service Issues**

Go to your **Admin** page in your Rent Manager account. Then open **System Web Preferences**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 11 10 2025 01 32 PM Pn](/images/Desktop-screenshot-11-10-2025_01_32_PM.png)
</Frame>

Select **Tenant Web Access** from the list on the left.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 11 10 2025 01 34 PM Pn](/images/Desktop-screenshot-11-10-2025_01_34_PM.png)
</Frame>

Select **Service Issues** from the options presented.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 11 10 2025 01 35 PM Pn](/images/Desktop-screenshot-11-10-2025_01_35_PM.png)
</Frame>

Uncheck the box labeled **Allow Tenant to Create Service Issues** to disable this feature, then click **Apply** and **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktopscreenshot Pn](/images/Desktopscreenshot.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  This option is only viable if Latchel is handling maintenance for **all units** in Rent Manager, as it applies a global change across the system.
</Warning>

### **Redirect Maintenance: Before Login to Rent Manager**

After disabling tenant access to create maintenance requests in Rent Manager, add a **login page banner** to redirect residents to Latchel. This ensures requests are submitted directly through Latchel for proper tracking and faster service.

Also under System Web Preferences, go to Tenant Web Access. Then click the pencil icon.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 11 14 2025 12 49 PM Pn](/images/Desktop-screenshot-11-14-2025_12_49_PM.png)
</Frame>

Then **add your personalized banner** as shown below. You can customize your banner however you like. Click **OK** to save.

<Tip>
  If you like to redirect customers to Latchel, just add your resident's work order URL by clicking **Insert Hyperlink**.
</Tip>

  ![Desktop Screenshot 11 14 2025 12 50 PM Pn](/images/Desktop-screenshot-11-14-2025_12_50_PM.png)
</Frame>
