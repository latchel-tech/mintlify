---
title: How to View Your Latchel-Assigned Phone Number
---


Your residents may call or text this phone number to create work orders, send in pictures, etc. As a Property Manager, you can also call the phone number issued to you to create new emergency requests.

To view the number, first of all, log in to your **Latchel portal**. 

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-99.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


Click on your initials or profile picture in the upper right-hand corner of the dashboard, and select **Account Settings.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-100.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


On the left-hand side of your page, click the drop-down for **Maint. Communication Settings. **There you will see the phone number Latchel has assigned to your account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-101.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
