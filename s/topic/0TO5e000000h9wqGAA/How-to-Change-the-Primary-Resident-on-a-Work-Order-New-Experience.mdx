Here's how you can change the primary resident on a work order:

In the work order page, click on the 3 dots next to the **Resident** modal and select **Edit Resident.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-862.jpg)
</Frame>

You will be prompted to **Select** or **Create New Resident.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-863.jpg)
</Frame>

Select the appropriate resident from the prompted list, then **Submit.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-864.jpg)
</Frame>

And that's how easy you can change a resident in a work order\!
