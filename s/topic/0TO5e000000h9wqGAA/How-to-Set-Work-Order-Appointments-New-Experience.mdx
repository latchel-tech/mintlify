---
title: Updating Work Order on Waiting For Materials Status (New Experience)
description: "This help guide will walk you through updating the schedule of a work order that is in a **Waiting for Materials** status. Additionally, it will show you how to send a reminder to the service provider if you are uncertain of the service provider's availability."
mode: wide
---

#### **Schedule Now**

From the work order page click **Schedule Now** located at the top right corner.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-242.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Adjust the date and time of the work date if needed, then hit **Submit.**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-243.jpg"/>
</Frame>

#### **Remind Service Provider**

If you have no idea about the availability of the service provider, click the **Remind Service Provider** on the upper right corner of the page.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-244.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From here you could add some additional details, or just hit the **Send** button.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-245.jpg"/>
</Frame>

And that's how you can update a work order on a waiting for materials status!
