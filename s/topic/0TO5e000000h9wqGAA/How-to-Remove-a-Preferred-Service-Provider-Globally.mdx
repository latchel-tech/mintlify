---
title: How to Remove a Preferred Service Provider (Globally)
description: "First, you need to visit your Service Provider page at **People** then **Service Providers**."
mode: wide
---


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-782.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Then the **My Service Provider Management** tab.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-783.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Look for the Service Provider that you wish to de-prioritize and click the three ellipses across its name. From the drop-down, choose **De-Prioritize Service Provider.**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-784.jpg"/>
</Frame>

This is the option to deprioritize a service provider globally. When you click this option, a warning message appears to confirm your action. Click **Choose** to proceed. 


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-785.jpg"/>
</Frame>
* If you agree to continue, the Service Provider will be deprioritized for all skills and categories in your Latchel portal.

<Info>
  **Note**: This help guide is different from blacklisting a Service Provider. Blacklisting a Service Provider will completely remove the Service Provider from a specific company to prevent them from being assigned to any work orders.
</Info>
