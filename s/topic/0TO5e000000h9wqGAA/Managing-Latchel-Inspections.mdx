### **Creating New Inspection**

To create new inspection, go to **Maintenance** and choose either **Inspections**.

If it's your first time creating one, you will find the image below. Click **Create Your First Inspection.**

<Frame>
  ![Image 06 30 2025 01 31 PM Pn](/images/image-06-30-2025_01_31_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  If you already have a list of inspections, click **Create New** at the top of the page.
</Tip>

Then, in the next page, click **Create Inspection**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 06 30 2025 01 33 PM Pn](/images/Desktop-screenshot-06-30-2025_01_33_PM.png)
</Frame>

Next, **choose an inspection template**, click **Next** once done.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 06 30 2025 01 50 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-06-30-2025_01_50_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  In this example, we will select **Blank**.
</Note>

In the next step, **search for the property** associated with the inspection, or click **Skip** if the inspection is not linked to any property.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 06 30 2025 02 00 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-06-30-2025_02_00_PM.png)
</Frame>

Enter a **name for the inspection** and provide a **brief description** of its purpose. Click **Create List** once you're finished.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 06 30 2025 02 03 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-06-30-2025_02_03_PM.png)
</Frame>

Congratulations\! You've successfully created your inspection. Click **My Inspections** to view the inspection you just created.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 06 30 2025 02 46 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-06-30-2025_02_46_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **Adding a New Item Within an Inspection**

If you need to capture specific details or areas unique to your property, follow these steps to add custom items to your existing inspection.

From your inspection list, click the \*\*hyperlinked title \*\*of the inspection you want to add new custom item.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 45 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_45_AM.png)
</Frame>

On the inspection page, click the **plus (\+) icon** located at the top right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 08 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_08_PM.png)
</Frame>

Then, **select the area**, **enter the item name and description**, and click **Add** once you're finished.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 09 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_09_PM.png)
</Frame>

A prompt will show that the Inspection item is added.

<Frame>
  ![Image 07 01 2025 12 13 PM Pn](/images/image-07-01-2025_12_13_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **Deleting an Inspections**

If an inspection you previously created is no longer needed, you can delete either the entire inspection list or specific items within it by following the steps below.

- **Deleting the Entire Inspection Order**

From your inspection list, click the **hyperlinked title** of the inspection you want to delete.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 45 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_45_AM.png)
</Frame>

Then, click the **trash icon** at the top of the page.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 46 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_46_AM.png)
</Frame>

When prompted, click **OK**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 47 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_47_AM.png)
</Frame>

The page will refresh, and you’ll see that the inspection has been removed from your list.

- **Deleting an Item within an Inspection**

If a specific item in your inspection order is no longer needed, you can remove it by following the steps

Navigate to your inspection page and **locate the item** you wish to delete.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 58 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_58_AM.png)
</Frame>

**Expand** the item, then click **Delete**. The item will be removed from the list.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 59 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_59_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Updating an Existing Inspection**

If you need to make changes to an in-progress inspection—edit details, property address, changing the inspection name, follow the steps below.

- **Update the Inspection Name**

From your inspection list, click the **hyperlinked title of the inspection** you want to update.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 11 45 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_11_45_AM.png)
</Frame>

To update the inspection title or name, **double-click the current name**, **make your changes**, and click **Save** when you're done.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 35 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_35_PM.png)
</Frame>

- **Update the Property Address**

If you need to update the property address associated to the inspection, first click the **pencil () icon**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 34 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_34_PM.png)
</Frame>

Next, **search for the property address** and click **Select** to proceed.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 41 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_41_PM.png)
</Frame>

The new address will automatically appear.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 42 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_42_PM.png)
</Frame>

- **Update/Edit the Inspection Description**

To update the inspection description, **double-click the current description**, **make your changes**, and click **Save** when you're done.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 50 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_50_PM.png)
</Frame>

- **Update/Edit an Inspection Item**

To update an inspection item, **locate the item** you need to edit, **expand it** to reveal the Edit button, then click **Edit**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 12 59 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_12_59_PM.png)
</Frame>

**Make the changes** you need—**area, name, or instructions**. Click **Update** once you're finish.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 07 01 2025 13 00 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-07-01-2025_13_00_PM.png)
</Frame>

The changes you made will automatically **appear** instantly.
