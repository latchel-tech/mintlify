---
title: "How to Override/Change Service Provider Payment Method in Portfolio"
mode: "wide"
description: "Learn how to override or update the payment method across an entire portfolio to streamline payments for all associated Service Providers."
---

On your Latchel dashboard, click **Places,** then on the dropdown select **Portfolios**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-749.jpg)
</Frame>

From the portfolio page either **select** a portfolio on the list below or **search** the portfolio that you need to edit.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-750.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Override Service Provider Payment Method**

On the portfolio page that you need to edit, go to the **Payments** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-751.jpg)
</Frame>

Under the Payment Settings section, click **Override** under Service Provider Payment Method.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-752.jpg)
</Frame>

Then on the pop-up window, **select** the payment option that you want to use.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-753.jpg)
</Frame>

And click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-754.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Change Service Provider Payment Method**

On the portfolio page that you need to edit, go to the **Payments** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-755.jpg)
</Frame>

Under the Payment Settings section, click the **ellipsis icon** for Service Provider Payment Method and then select **Change Payment Method**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-756.jpg)
</Frame>

Then on the pop-up window, **select** the payment option that you want to use.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-757.jpg)
</Frame>

And click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-758.jpg)
</Frame>
