title: "How to Tag Video Calls to a Work Order"
description: "Similar to phone calls, a video can be tagged to a work order too."
mode: "wide"
---

The purpose of this help guide is to ensure interaction with the resident will be recorded on the specific work order and here's how:

From the work order page, scroll down to reach the resident lists and click the **video call icon** of the intended resident.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-531.jpg)
</Frame>

Notice that the work order title will be visible for the video to be tagged, then click **Create Video Chat Room.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-532.jpg)
</Frame>

The video call will progress, and the resident should be available on the other line to share the maintenance issue.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-533.jpg)
</Frame>

To finish the call, click on the **red headphone** icon, thenÂ **Close Window** to fully close the video call.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-534.jpg)
</Frame>

Once the video session ends, a URL and the link to the **View Call Log** will be available to copy. For this help guide, click on **View Call Log.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-535.jpg)
</Frame>

The page will be routed to the **"Call information"** page. The call recording or video information will be available, and this is automatically tagged or associated with the work order.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-536.jpg)
</Frame>
