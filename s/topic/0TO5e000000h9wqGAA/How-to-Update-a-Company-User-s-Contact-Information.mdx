---
title: How to Update a Company User's Contact Information
description: "After you logged in to Latchel, click the **arrow down** beside your user icon at the top right corner of your dashboard. Then, click **Account Settings**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-703.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From the **Account Settings** page, click **Users**. Then, click the **name of the user** you need to update the contact information.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-704.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Once you click the user's name, it will take you to the **Member's Profile page**. From there, you can update the user's phone number and/or email address.Click **Save** to save the changes you made.

**Note:** _You can also add secondary contact information if the user or property manager has multiple phone numbers and email addresses._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-705.jpg"/>
</Frame>

And there you go! That's how easily you can update any of your user's contact information!
