---
title: How to Set Service Provider as a VIP Preferred Service Provider
description: "If the Service Provider is set as VIP it can accept a job without scheduling an arrival time."
mode: wide
---

Go to your Latchel Dashboard and click on **People**. Then select **Service Providers**Â from the dropdown menu.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-654.jpg"/>
</Frame>

On the Service Providers page, click on **My Service Provider Management**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-655.jpg"/>
</Frame>
Now choose which Service Provider you want to set as VIP and select the **3 dots** on the right of your selected Service Provider. Then click **Preferred Service Provider Settings.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-656.jpg"/>
</Frame>
<br/>
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-657.jpg"/>
</Frame>

In the pop-up window, you will see the **VIP Service Provider** option at the bottom. Simply switch the toggle button to **ON** or **OFF** to set or remove the VIP status for that service provider.
