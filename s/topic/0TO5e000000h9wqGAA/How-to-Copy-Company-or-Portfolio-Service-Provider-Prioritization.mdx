---
title: "How to Copy Company or Portfolio Service Provider Prioritization"
description: "This guide will provide you with the steps on how to copy the Service Provider prioritization of the company or the portfolio."
mode: wide
---

To update a property's Service Provider Prioritization with its parent Company or Portfolio's Service Providers and priorities, follow these steps:

First, navigate to the **Places** tab and select **Properties.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-460.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Then, choose the desired property either by searching or clicking on it.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-461.jpg"/>
</Frame>


On the property page, click on the **Service Provider** tab.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-462.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Click the **ellipsis icon** across the **Service Provider Prioritization.**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-463.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Select **Copy Company Priorities** to replace all Service Providers and priorities in the property with those in the parent Company. If the property is in the  Portfolio, all Service Providers prioritized will be replaced with those in the parent Portfolio.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-464.jpg"/>
</Frame>


<Info>

**NOTE:**_If this option is turned OFF, this will automatically disable the inheritance feature for the selected property, which means that any changes made to the parent Company's Service Providers and priorities will **NOT** affect this property's Service provider prioritization._
</Info>
