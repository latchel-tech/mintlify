---
title: How to Re-Enable a Property
description: "**To re-enable old or disabled property, follow these steps:**"
mode: wide
---
On the upper left-hand side of your page, click on the **Places** tab and select **Properties.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-896.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Once you are on the Properties page, click on **Show Disabled Properties.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-897.jpg"/>
</Frame>
Select the property you want to re-enable using the **search bar**. Then, click the hyperlinked property address once it shows.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-898.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From the property page, click the **three dots** at the upper-right corner of the page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-899.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Click on **Enable Property.**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-900.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Click **Submit** to save.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-901.jpg"/>
</Frame>


That's it! The property is now **Enabled**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-902.jpg"/>
</Frame>
