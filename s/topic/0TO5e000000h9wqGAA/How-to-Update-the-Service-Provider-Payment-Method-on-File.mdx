---
title: How to Update the Service Provider Payment Method on File
---

On the Latchel portal click the account icon dropdown and select **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-94.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


In the Account Setting page on the **Payment Settings** tab, go to the **Payment Methods** then select **Add Payment Method**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-95.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


In the Add Payment Method, select the type of payment -- **Credit/Debit Card** or **Bank Account**. Then add the required details depending on the payment method you choose.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-96.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


If your preferred payment method is already added, go to the **Payment Settings** located under the Payment Methods section. From there, you can set the **Service Provider Payment Method**. Click **ellipsis** on the Payment Settings, and choose either **Change Payment Method** or **Remove Payment Method**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-97.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


If you choose **Change Payment Method,** select another payment method from the dropdown option and click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-98.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
