---
title: "End SMS Conversation Without Assigning It to Property Managers"
description: "There are situations where a resident might just respond with **Thank you** and **doesn't require a response** from the Property Manager."
mode: wide
---

From the **SMS box,** select the **Ellipsis icon** on the top right and click **End Conversation** from the Dropdown.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-604.jpg"/>
</Frame>

A reminder will show and ask if you want to tag the conversation, just click **No.** Once you have clicked No, confirm the action by clicking **Yes**.
