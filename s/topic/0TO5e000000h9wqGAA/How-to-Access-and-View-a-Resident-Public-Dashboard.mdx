title: "How to Access and View a Resident Public Dashboard"
mode: "wide"
description: "This guide covers how to access and view a resident's Public Dashboard. The dashboard gives you a real-time view of a resident’s work order, including updates, scheduling details, and communication history—making it easy to stay informed and provide support when needed."
---

From your dashboard click **People**, and select **Residents**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/1Latchel-04-15-2025_08_36_AM.png)
</Frame>

You can **search** for the resident or just **select** from the list.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/2Latchel-04-15-2025_08_37_AM.png)
</Frame>

Once you have selected the resident, go to the **Miscellaneous** section at the lower left part of the page.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/3Latchel-04-15-2025_08_39_AM.png)
</Frame>

From there you can **grab/copy** the link under the Public Dashboard URL. **Paste** the link to your web browser's address bar and it will lead you to the resident's dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![4latchel 04 15 2025 08 42 AM Pn](/images/4Latchel-04-15-2025_08_42_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Every public dashboard URL is unique for each of the residents. For more information about resident dashboard, please check [**Resident Dashboard Overview.**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Resident-Dashboard-Overview)
</Tip>
