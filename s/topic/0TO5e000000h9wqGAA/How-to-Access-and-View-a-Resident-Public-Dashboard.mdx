---
title: How to Access and View a Resident Public Dashboard
description: "From your dashboard click **People** and select **Residents**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-192.jpg"/>
</Frame>

You can **search** for the resident or just **select** from the list.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-193.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Once you have selected the resident, go to the **Miscellaneous** section at the lower left part of the page.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-194.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From there you can **grab/copy** the link under the Public Dashboard URL. **Paste** the link to your web browser's address bar and it will lead you to the resident's dashboard.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-195.jpg"/>
</Frame>

<Info>
  **Note:** _Every public dashboard URL is unique for each of the residents._
</Info>

For more information about resident dashboard, please check **[Resident Dashboard Overview](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/Resident-Dashboard-Overview).**
