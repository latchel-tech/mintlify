---
title: How to Export Your Invoice Data to a CSV
description: "Here's how you can export your invoice data in Latchel:"
mode: wide
---

From your dashboard, click on **Invoices** from the top.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-796.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Next, click **Export** in the top right corner.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-797.jpg"/>
</Frame>

A pop-up will then appear asking if you want to download all of the invoices to a CSV file. Click **Ok**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-798.jpg"/>
</Frame>
Depending on your computer browser settings, you will be asked to save the CSV file or it will automatically download. Access the CSV file from your computer downloads folder once done downloading.
