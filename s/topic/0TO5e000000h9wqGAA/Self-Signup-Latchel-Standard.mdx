#### **How To Sign Up**

To create your account, navigate to the [Latchel-Signup](https://app.latchel.com/signup) page. A confirmation email will be sent to your email address after your successful signup that includes steps on how to schedule an onboarding call and how to access the portal.

<Icon icon="circle-1" iconType="sharp-solid" color="#ff9b8a" /> **Will I be charged for the subscription after signing up?**

Charges on your card will only apply **_after the 30-day free trial_** is complete. You can also cancel at any time with no pain\!

<Icon icon="circle-3" iconType="sharp-solid" color="#ff9b8a" /> **How can I cancel the subscription if it's no longer needed?**

As mentioned, you can **_cancel the trial period at any time_**\! Just follow the simple steps below.

**Cancelling Your Subscription**

Access the **Account Settings**, and under the **Subscription** tab, a button exists, then hit **Cancel Free Trial**. This will automatically log you out of Latchel and confirm you won’t be charged.
