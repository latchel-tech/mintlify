#### **How To Sign Up**

To create your account, navigate to the [Latchel-Signup](https://app.latchel.com/signup) page. A confirmation email will be sent to your email address after your successful signup that includes steps on how to schedule an onboarding call and how to access the portal.

Access the **Account Settings**, and under the **Subscription** tab, a button exists, then hit **Cancel Free Trial**. This will automatically log you out of Latchel and confirm you won’t be charged.
