---
title: Integrating Rent Manager into Latchel
---

<Note>
Please review our **[Rent Manager Quick Reference Guide](https://latchel.com/blog/rent-manager-integration-reference-guide/)** prior to requesting to activate the integration.
</Note>

**To integrate Rent Manager with Latchel, follow these simple steps:**

Log into your **Rent Manager** Account, and go to **Available Integrations** located in the **Admin** tab.
 
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screen-Shot-2020-06-24-at-11.webp" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


Search for the Latchel integration, then click **Request Activation**.

 
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screen_Shot_2020_06_24_at_1_17_21_PM.webp" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>



Fill out all of the information in the form and then click **Request activation**.

 
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screen_Shot_2020_06_24_at_1_22_47_PM.webp" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>



Latchel will get an email with your activation request. Once it is approved, you can see it in **My Integrations** in the **Admin** tab.

 
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screen-Shot-2020-06-24-at-11-1.webp" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


Well done! Your Rent Manager is now integrated with Latchel. For more questions about this, you may also contact your Customer Success Manager via [success@latchel.com](mailto:success@latchel.com).
