#### **Set Up Automatic Background Sync**

With the help of this option, you can easily keep your data up to date by having data synchronization run in the background on an hourly or daily basis.

1. **Hourly Background Sync**

Switch the option **Automatic, hourly background sync** to **ON** by moving the toggle to the right.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-217.jpg)
</Frame>

2. **Daily Background Sync**

Switch the option **Automatic, daily background sync** to **ON** by moving the toggle to the right.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-218.jpg)
</Frame>

Then, **choose the time of the day** you would like the sync to happen.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-219.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **How to Sync All Data Manually**

For those who prefer manual control, the Latchel Data Sync Extension also offers an option to manually sync all data whenever needed.

To do this, click **Sync All Data**. This allows your property management software to sync all data to Latchel to keep your records up to date.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-220.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **How to Download Debug Log**

A **Debug Log** is used to record detailed information about the execution of a program or extension, including errors and operational data. This information is essential for troubleshooting issues, diagnosing problems, and improving the overall performance and stability of the software.

To download this, click **Download Debug Log**. Click **Save** on your computer if prompted.
