---
title: "How to Manage My Own Invoice Notification Settings"
description: "Stay in control of your billing alerts by customizing your invoice notification settings. This guide will show you how to adjust your preferences so you receive updates the way that works best for you."
mode: "wide"
---

On the upper right corner of the Latchel dashboard, click your **user icon** and on the dropdown select **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  ![1latchel Pn](/images/1Latchel.png)
</Frame>

Under the Details tab click the dropdown icon of **Maint. Communication Settings** to expand the options.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 15 2025 05 51 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-05-15-2025_05_51_PM.png)
</Frame>

From there, you can manage the toggle option for **Send invoice notifications** and choose who should receive the notification by **checking the corresponding box** of one of the default options and/or **adding a specific email address**. Finally, click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 15 2025 05 54 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-05-15-2025_05_54_PM.png)
</Frame>
