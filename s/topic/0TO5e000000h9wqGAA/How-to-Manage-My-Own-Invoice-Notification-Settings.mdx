---
title: How to Manage My Own Invoice Notification Settings
description: "On the right corner of the Latchel dashboard, click your **initial icon** and on the dropdown select **Account Settings**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-745.jpg"/>
</Frame>
Under the Details tab click the dropdown icon of **Maint. Communication Settings** to expand the options.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-746.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From there you could manage the toggle option of **Send invoice notifications**, and select who you would like to send the invoice notification to by checking the corresponding box of one of the default options and/or adding a specific email.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-747.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Then hit **Save**.

  <img src="/images/rtaImage-748.jpg"/>
</Frame>
