---
title: How to Add a Property
---


If you do not have a system integration in place, it is important to know how to add new properties through the Latchel portal.

On the upper left-hand side of your dashboard, click **Places** then **Properties.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-91.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


On the upper right-hand side of the screen, click **New Property.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-92.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>


Enter the property details & click **Save!**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-2-93.jpg" alt="image.png"/>
</Frame>
