---
title: "How to Add a Property"
description: "If you do not have a system integration in place, it’s important to know how to add new properties directly through the Latchel portal. Follow these steps to add a new property. "
---

On the upper left-hand side of your dashboard, click **Places** then **Properties.**

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-2-91.jpg)
</Frame>

On the upper right-hand side of the screen, click **New Property.**

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-2-92.jpg)
</Frame>

Enter the property details & click **Save\!**

<Frame>
  ![image.png](/images/rtaImage-2-93.jpg)
</Frame>
