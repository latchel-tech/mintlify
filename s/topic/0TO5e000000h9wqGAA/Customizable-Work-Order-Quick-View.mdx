---
title: Customizable Work Order Quick View
description: "Now Property Managers can customize their default view for the work order detail page. By choosing which most relevant information to show and how they want to see it in order."
mode: wide
---

From your Latchel portal open any **work order** to configure the quick view.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-724.jpg"/>
</Frame>

In the work order, you will notice the **Quick View** tab on the left side of the page. Where the page is initially empty and can be customized based on the information or workflow.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-725.jpg"/>
</Frame>

To personalize your Quick View, click the **Configure** button in the center of the page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-726.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Now in the configuration window, you will see the list of work order details that are initially turned off. Then click the **Eye icon** next to the work order detail that you want to add to your quick view.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-727.jpg"/>
</Frame>


You can also adjust the order of the details, by clicking and holding the **Handlebars icon** of the corresponding detail, then drag and drop it into your preferred place and sequence.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-728.jpg"/>
</Frame>



_**Note:** All the changes that you make in the configure view are automatically updated and the configuration you make will be applied to all of your work orders._



If you need to change your **Quick View tab**, just open a work order and click the **Configure** button on the top right corner of the page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-729.jpg"/>
</Frame>
