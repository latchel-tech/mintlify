---
title: Property Manager 360 Degree Communication View
description: "Introducing the new **360 Degree SMS communication view**! Property managers can now have enhanced visibility into the SMS conversations that take place between Latchel associates and their owners, residents, or service providers."
mode: wide
---

This new feature allows property managers to stay informed and engaged, ensuring seamless communication throughout the entire property management process. Here's how:


Click the **ellipses** menu in the headers of the SMS window.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-815.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Choose **Latchel Agent Conversations** from the drop-down, this will pull up a view-only transcript of all SMS Conversations that Latchel Operations has had with the person.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-816.jpg"/>
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-817.jpg"/>
</Frame>

<Info>
**Note:**

Only conversations that have been tagged to a Property Manager company or a work order for the Property Management Company will show up in the transcript.

Latchel Agents will ensure not to forget to tag a conversation otherwise they cannot go back and tag SMS once the conversation has ended.

</Info>
