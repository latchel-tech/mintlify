### **Create a Schedule Export**

From your Analytics dashboard, click the folder icon at the bottom-left corner of the page, then select **Schedule export** from the list of options.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 03 45 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_03_45_AM.png)
</Frame>

Click **Create new schedule**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2025 10 15 034102 10 15 2025 03 47 AM Pn](/images/Screenshot-2025-10-15-034102-10-15-2025_03_47_AM.png)
</Frame>

In the Schedule exports window, add the **recipient’s email address**. You can also **customize** the **subject and message** that will be sent to the recipient.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 03 48 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_03_48_AM.png)
</Frame>

Select which Analytics **report**, and the **file format**. You can add as **many reports** as you'd like.

<Note>
  Page layout and size options will only appear if the file type is set to PDF.
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Image 10 15 2025 03 50 AM Pn](/images/image-10-15-2025_03_50_AM.png)
</Frame>

Next, set the **Frequency** — choose whether you want the report to be sent **daily, weekly, or monthly**, and **specify the time of day**. You can also **select the time zone**, if needed.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 03 53 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_03_53_AM.png)
</Frame>

You can also **set conditions** that must be met before a report is exported. By default, it's set to **always**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 03 55 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_03_55_AM.png)
</Frame>

If conditions are set, feel free to click **Test condition** to receive a test email.

<Frame>
  ![Test Condition Gi](/images/TestCondition.gif)
</Frame>

Once all settings are set, click **Create **to save the new schedule.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 04 01 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_04_01_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Manage Existing Scheduled Exports**

If you have active scheduled reports, you can still modify their settings. Here’s how:

Go to your **scheduled exports** and **select** **the one** you want to update. Click the **ellipsis** under Action. Then, select **Edit**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2025 10 15 034139 10 15 2025 04 48 AM Pn](/images/Screenshot-2025-10-15-034139-10-15-2025_04_48_AM.png)
</Frame>

**Modify** any of the current settings, click **Save** once done.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 04 49 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_04_49_AM.png)
</Frame>

**In addition to Edit, you can also:**

- Rename Schedule
- Send now
- Pause
- Delete
- Duplicate

And that's how easy you can set a scheduled export!
