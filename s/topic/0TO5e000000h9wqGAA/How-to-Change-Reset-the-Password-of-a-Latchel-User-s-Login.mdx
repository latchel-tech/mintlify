---
title: How to Change/Reset the Password of a Latchel User's Login
description: "To change your password, click the **arrow down** beside your user icon at the top right corner of your dashboard. Then, select **Account Settings**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-696.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From the **Account Settings** page, click** Users.** The list of company users will appear. From there, select the user with the email you wish to reset the password.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-697.jpg"/>
</Frame>

<Info>
  **Note:** _The email address of the user selected is the email address that will be notified for a password reset. If you need to update the user's email address, follow the steps in_
</Info>

In the **User or Member profile page**, click the **circle with three dots** at the upper right corner.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-698.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Once clicked, there are **two options** to select, **Send login email** and **Disable user.** Click **Send login email.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-699.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Once done, the user of the email will receive an email from Latchel to reset the password.


Click the **Create Password** button in the email.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-700.jpg"/>
</Frame>

The button will take the user to the **Latchel webpage** where the password can be changed. From there, type the new password.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-701.jpg"/>
</Frame>

If the New Password and the Confirm Password match, the **Submit Password** buttonwill be available. Click the **Submit Password** to log in.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-702.jpg"/>
</Frame>
And that's it! The user will be redirected to the Latchel dashboard with the new password!
