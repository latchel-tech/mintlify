---
title: "How to Enable or Disable a Resident in Latchel"
description: "This help guide will provide property managers with the necessary steps to enable or disable residents in Latchel, as well as outline the status with integrations."
---

### **Searching and Enabling Residents**

From the top navigation menu click **People**, then on the dropdown select **Residents**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-769.jpg)
</Frame>

On the residents page check the **Show Disabled Accounts** box to display all resident profiles. Now in the **Search Field**, you could use either the resident's name, email, or phone number to search for the resident's profile.

Once searched, click on the **Name** of the resident.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-770.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  A disabled resident's account will have an **"Account Disabled"** tag displayed under their name.
</Note>

Now in the resident's profile, click on the **ellipsis icon** (three dots) across the resident's name then select **Enable Account**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-771.jpg)
</Frame>

And click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-772.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Disabling Residents**

In the resident's profile, click on the **ellipsis icon** (three dots) across the resident's name then select **Disable Account**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-773.jpg)
</Frame>

And click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-774.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  This action is **only** applicable to accounts **without an integration** unless the Property Manager has provided a report with a list of residents that require a status change.
</Note>
