---
title: How to Export Properties
description: "Log in to your Latchel account, click **Places**, then choose **Properties**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-800.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Click **Export** in the top right corner.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-801.jpg"/>
</Frame>

You'll then receive a pop-up asking if you want to download all the properties as a CSV file. Select **Ok**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-802.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Depending on your browser settings, either you will be asked to save the CSV file to download, or it will automatically save on your computer. Then, access the file from your computer downloads folder.
