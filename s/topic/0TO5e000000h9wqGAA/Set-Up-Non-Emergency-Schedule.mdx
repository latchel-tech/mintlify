Here's how you can set this up.

Log in to your Latchel Dashboard, then go to **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  ![1latchel Pn](/images/1Latchel.png)
</Frame>

On the **Details** tab, look for **Non-Emergency Scheduling**, then expand the section.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 22 2025 10 03 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-05-22-2025_10_03_AM.png)
</Frame>

**Toggle** the switch to the **right**; it will turn blue. Then, click **Save** to complete.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 22 2025 10 05 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-05-22-2025_10_05_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Turning this toggle on will prevent residents from adding evening availability across the company.
</Warning>
