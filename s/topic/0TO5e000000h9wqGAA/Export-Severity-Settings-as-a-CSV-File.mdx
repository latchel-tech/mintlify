---
title: "Export Severity Settings as a CSV File"
description: "If you would like to export a CSV version of your company's severity settings, you can do so with the following steps."
mode: wide
---

On your Latchel dashboard click your initials on the top right-hand corner, and on the drop select **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-812.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Then on the **Severity/Responsibility** tab, click the **Export** icon.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-813.jpg"/>
</Frame>

And click **OK.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-814.jpg"/>
</Frame>
_Once the download is complete, please check your **computer downloads** to access the (.)CSV file._
