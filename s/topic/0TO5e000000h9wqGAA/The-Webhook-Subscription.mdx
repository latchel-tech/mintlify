description: "Property managers can now receive webhook data when a **job**, **portfolio**, **property**, **resident**, **invoice**, **file**, and **owner** is either created or updated."
mode: "wide"
---

From the Latchel dashboard click the **Account Dropdown** icon located at the upper right corner, and select **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  ![1latchel Pn](/images/1Latchel.png)
</Frame>

In the Account Settings page click the **Integrations** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-632.jpg)
</Frame>

Scroll down to the Webhook section, then click the **New Webhook icon.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-633.jpg)
</Frame>

Then on the pop-up window set the desired **Model**, **Event**, and the **URL** that the webhook developer will provide.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-634.jpg)
</Frame>

<br />

<br />

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-635.jpg)
</Frame>

Set the toggle to **Active**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-636.jpg)
</Frame>
