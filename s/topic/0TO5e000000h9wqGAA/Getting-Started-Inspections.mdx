title: "Inspections"
description: " Whether you’re managing a unit turn, documenting property conditions, or performing routine check-ins, Latchel’s Inspections feature gives you the tools to capture everything in one place—efficiently and clearly. With customizable templates, mobile-friendly inspection forms, and the ability to create work orders directly from flagged items, your team can stay organized and on track from start to finish. Each inspection can be linked to a project for full visibility, helping you manage tasks, timelines, and property updates without missing a beat. In this guide, you’ll learn how to build templates, assign and complete inspections, and turn inspection results into actionable tasks—all while keeping your workflow smooth and your team aligned."
