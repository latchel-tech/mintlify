---
title: "How to Remove a Property"
description: "Follow these steps to remove a property from your Latchel account when it's no longer under your management."
mode: "wide"
---

**There are 2 ways to remove a property:**

- **Now**: removes the property immediately.
- **On a Future Date**: removes the property on the date you will set.

On the upper left-hand side of your dashboard, click **Places** then select **Properties.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-889.jpg)
</Frame>

Search for the property and click it.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-890.jpg)
</Frame>

On the upper right-hand side, click the **3 horizontal dots**, and select **Disable Property.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-891.jpg)
</Frame>

You have two options to disable the property by selecting **Now**, or **On a Future Date**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-892.jpg)
</Frame>

**Option 1**. If you select **Now**, click **Submit** to save. This disables the property **immediately.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-893.jpg)
</Frame>

**Option 2**. If you select **On a Future Date,** please set your desired date on when the property should be disabled. Click **Next** then **Submit** to save. **Once the date you selected arrives, the property will be automatically disabled**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-894.jpg)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-895.jpg)
</Frame>
