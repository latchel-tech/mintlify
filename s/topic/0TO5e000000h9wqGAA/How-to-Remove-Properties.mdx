---
title: How to Remove a Property
description: "On the upper left-hand side of your dashboard, click **Places** then select **Properties.**"
mode: wide
---


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-889.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Search for the property and click it.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-890.jpg"/>
</Frame>


On the upper right-hand side, click the **3 horizontal dots**, and select **Disable Property.**
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-891.jpg"/>
</Frame>


You have two options to disable the property by selecting **Now**, or **On a Future Date**.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-892.jpg"/>
</Frame>


**Option 1**. If you select **Now**, click **Submit** to save. This disables the property **immediately.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-893.jpg"/>
</Frame>


**Option 2**. If you select **On a Future Date,** please set your desired date on when the property should be disabled. Click **Next** then **S****ubmit** to save. **Once the date you selected arrives, the property will be automatically disabled**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-894.jpg"/>
</Frame>
