On your dashboard you will notice that there is a new **Tools** menu, nested in this menu you will find the section for **Invoices**, **Skill Categories**, and **Analytics**.

<Frame>
  ![Image 05 15 2025 02 29 PM Pn](/images/image-05-15-2025_02_29_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **Invoices**

In this section, you can view all of the Invoices that are related to your work orders.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-628.jpg)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  To know more about managing your invoices, check [Methods of Searching Invoices](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wqGAA/How-to-View-and-Search-for-Invoices).
</Tip>
