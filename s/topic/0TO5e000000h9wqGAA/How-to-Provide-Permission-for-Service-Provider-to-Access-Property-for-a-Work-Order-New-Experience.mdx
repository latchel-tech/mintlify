---
title: "How to Provide Permission for Service Provider to Access Property for a Work Order (New Experience)"
description: "From your Dashboard, click the **Maintenance** dropdown and select **Work Orders**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-418.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Now to select the desired work order, use the **Search Bar** then click on the **Work Order ID** on the **Work Order page**. You can also use **Filter** for easy search.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-419.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Next, go to **Details** located on the left side of the page,and navigate to the Property Access section on the right-hand side of the work order page. Then toggle the **Permission for Service Provider to Access Property** to **ON**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-420.jpg"/>
</Frame>
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-421.jpg"/>
</Frame>
Finally, provide clear access instructions for the service provider. Click **Save**. This will skip the automation for obtaining your resident’s availability for the work order.

  <img src="/images/rtaImage-422.jpg"/>
</Frame>
