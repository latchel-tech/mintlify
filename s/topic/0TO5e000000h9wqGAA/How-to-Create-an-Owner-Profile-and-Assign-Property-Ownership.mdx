---
title: How to Create an Owner Profile and Assign Property Ownership
description: "On the dashboard click the **Places** dropdown, then select **Properties**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-870.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Select the property.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-871.jpg"/>
</Frame>

In the Property Details tab, scroll down to the **Owner** section, and click on the three dots to open the menu with the option to **Change Owner**. Click on it.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-872.jpg"/>
</Frame>

You will be asked to either **Search for an existing owner** or to **Create New Owner**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-873.jpg"/>
</Frame>

If the owner is already in the system, type their name, phone number, or email address. and you will see their information showing up on the search bar. Click on it, then click **Submit**.

If the owner is not yet in the system, complete the details and contact information of the new owner. And click **Continue.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-874.jpg"/>
</Frame>

A confirmation screen will be displayed. Verify the information and click on **Submit**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-875.jpg"/>
</Frame>
<Info>
  **Note:** All email addresses associated with the owner's profile will receive any type of owner notification.
</Info>
