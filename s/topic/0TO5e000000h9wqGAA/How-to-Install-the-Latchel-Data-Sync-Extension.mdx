---

To install the Latchel Data Sync Chrome Extension, go to the Latchel Data Sync Chrome extension page [**here**](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/cefobanegfgmcneibgdbcjbegnnoafob) and click on **Add to Chrome**.

<Frame>
  ![1latchel Data Sync Chrome Web Store 04 29 2025 08 45 AM Pn](/images/1Latchel-Data-Sync-Chrome-Web-Store-04-29-2025_08_45_AM.png)
</Frame>

Once successfully installed, the Latchel extension will appear at the top right-hand side of the Chrome browser. Click on the Latchel extension and log in using your **Latchel** login credentials. To reset your password, click [**here.**](https://stage.latchel.com/forgot-password)

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-355.jpg)
</Frame>

That’s it\! The next steps must be completed with Latchel’s Customer Success team. Please let your onboarding representative know you have completed this guide's steps.
