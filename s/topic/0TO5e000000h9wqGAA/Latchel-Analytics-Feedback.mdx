---
title: "Latchel Analytics: Provide Feedback"
description: "Find out how to share your feedback directly within Latchel Analytics to help improve reporting features and overall performance."
---

Access your **Analytics dashboard**, then click **Provide analytics feedback** at the top-right corner of the page.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 10 15 2025 05 20 AM Pn](/images/Latchel-10-15-2025_05_20_AM.png)
</Frame>

This will take you to a **Typeform**. Just answer all questions asked, then click **Submit** once done! For more urgent matters, you may email [success@latchel.com](mailto:success@latchel.com).
