First, **search for the work order** you need to update. From your **Dashboard**, click the **Maintenance** dropdown and select **Work Orders**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-406.jpg)
</Frame>

Now to select the desired work order, use the **Search Bar** then click on the **Work Order ID** on the Work Order page. You can also use **Filter** for easy search.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-407.jpg)
</Frame>

### **How to Edit/Change the Service Provider Assigned on a Work Order**

Once you are in the work order select **Quick View** or **Details** tab, then look for the **Service Provider** section.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-408.jpg)
</Frame>

From there click the ellipsis <Icon icon="circle-ellipsis" iconType="sharp-regular" size={25} />, then choose **Edit SP**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-409.jpg)
</Frame>

You may select from the list of the **Service Providers** for Work Order's required skill. Or **Search** for the Service Provider company if the service provider is not on the list, then click **Select**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-410.jpg)
</Frame>

Once you have chosen the Service Provider, fill out the box providing details of why you are changing the service provider, and click **Add to Work Order.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-411.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  If there are multiple users in the service provider company, please also select the user to be assigned in the work order.
</Note>

### **How to Remove the Service Provider Assigned on a Work Order**

From the Service Provider section click the **ellipsis **<Icon icon="circle-ellipsis" iconType="sharp-regular" size={25} /> and choose **Remove SP** if you need to completely remove or unassign the service provider from the work order. 

<Warning>
  Removing a service provider assigned to the work order will **not** prompt you to assign or choose a different service provider. If you wish to re-assign another service provider, choose **Edit SP** instead.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-412.jpg)
</Frame>

Next, leave a note summarizing why you are removing the service provider. Then click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-413.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  When a service provider is unassigned from the work order, the work order will be changed to Finding Service Provider status.
</Note>
