Now to select the desired work order, use the **Search Bar** then click on the **Work Order ID** on the Work Order page. You can also use **Filter** for easy search.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-407.jpg)
</Frame>

#### **How to Edit/Change the Service Provider Assigned on a Work Order**

Once you are in the work order select **Quick View** or **Details** tab, then look for the **Service Provider** section.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-408.jpg)
</Frame>

From there hit the **Elipsis icon** and click **Edit SP**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-409.jpg)
</Frame>

<Info>
  **NOTE:**\_ You **Cannot** Change the Service Provider if the status is set to **Troubleshooting.**\_
</Info>

You may select from the list of the **Service Providers** for Work Order's required skill. Or **Search** for the Service Provider company if the service provider is not on the list, then click **Select**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-410.jpg)
</Frame>

Once you have chosen the Service Provider, fill out the box providing details of why you are changing the service provider, and click **Add to Work Order.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-411.jpg)
</Frame>

<Info>
  **NOTE:** _If there are multiple users in the service provider company, please also select the user to be assigned in the work order._
</Info>

#### **How to Remove the Service Provider Assigned on a Work Order**

From the Service Provider section click the **Ellipsis icon** and choose **Remove SP**,if you need to completely remove or unassign the service provider from the work order. Removing a service provider assigned to the work order will not prompt you to assign or choose a different service provider. If you wish to re-assign another service provider, choose Edit SP instead.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-412.jpg)
</Frame>

Next, leave a note summarizing why you are removing the service provider. Then click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-413.jpg)
</Frame>

<Info>
  **NOTE:** _When a service provider is unassigned from the work order, the work order will be changed to Finding Service Provider status._
</Info>
