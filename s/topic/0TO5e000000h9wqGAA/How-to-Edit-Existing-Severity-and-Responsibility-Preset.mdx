---
title: How to Edit Existing Severity and Responsibility Preset
description: "From your Latchel Dashboard, click the **initials of your account** located at the top right corner. And on the dropdown, select **Account Settings**."
mode: wide
---

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-624.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Then on the **Severity/Responsibility** tab, you can change the severity preset for each of the categories. Using the dropdown **Settings Type**, or the **Problem Category**, you can filter the responsibility you wish to update.
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-625.jpg"/>
</Frame>
Click the dropdown to show the options. Choose if the settings for each of the severity/responsibility should be under **Normal**, **Urgent**, **Emergency**, **Needs Pre-Approval**, and **Resident's Responsibility**.
