---
title: "Adding Your Company Address"
description: "Keeping your company address up to date in Latchel ensures accurate invoicing and proper documentation. This guide walks you through how to add or update your company address in just a few steps."
---

Access your **Account Settings** by clicking your **user icon** at the far right corner of your dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![1latchel Pn](/images/1Latchel.png)
</Frame>

Expand **Address**, then add or update your company address. Click **Save** once done.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 15 2025 03 32 PM Pn](/images/Latchel-05-15-2025_03_32_PM.png)
</Frame>

And that's how easy you can update your company address on file\!
