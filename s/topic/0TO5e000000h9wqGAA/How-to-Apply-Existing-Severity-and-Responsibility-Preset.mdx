---
title: How to Apply Existing Severity and Responsibility Preset
description: "Applying an existing severity and responsibility is applicable when you have previous settings that were already saved."
mode: wide
---

From your Latchel Dashboard, click the **initial of your account** located at the top right corner. And on the dropdown, select **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-804.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Then on the **Severity/Responsibility** tab, click the **ellipsis** icon and select **Apply Settings Set**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-805.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Now on the pop-up window choose a **Settings Set** from the dropdown and click **Apply Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-806.jpg"/>
</Frame>
