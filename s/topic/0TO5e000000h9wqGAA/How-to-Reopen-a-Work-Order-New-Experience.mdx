Once you are on the work order page, just click the **Reopen Work Order** option in the notification located in the right-hand corner of the page.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-301.jpg)
</Frame>

Then a pop-up window will appear, from there you can add some additional details or just hit **Submit** and you're done.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-302.jpg)
</Frame>

After initiating the work order update, you can verify its status to confirm the changes. The work order will now move to **Rescheduling Resident** or **Scheduling Service Provider** status.
