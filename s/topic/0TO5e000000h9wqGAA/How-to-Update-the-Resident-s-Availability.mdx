---
title: "How to Update the Resident's Availability"
description: "As a property manager you can either grant the service provider access to the property if the resident is not available, send a reminder of the work order, or add another schedule for the resident. Here are the steps on how you can do it on a work order."
---

### **Grant Property Access**

From the work order page hit the **Grant Property Access** in the notification located at the upper right-hand corner of the page.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-579.jpg)
</Frame>

Then on the pop-up screen, you can provide additional instructions and click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-580.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Remind a Resident**

If you need to send an email or SMS to a resident to remind them of a pending task that requires their action on a work order, follow the steps below.

Also located at the upper right corner of the work order page, click theÂ **Remind Resident** button.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-581.jpg)
</Frame>

And from the pop-up screen, you can edit the reminder message or just hit **Send**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-582.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Add Resident Availability**

Click the **Hide** button of the floating section located in the upper right corner.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-583.jpg)
</Frame>

From there, select **Other Actions**, then click **Add Resident Availability**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-584.jpg)
</Frame>

Now you can choose from the available schedule, or custom a specific time slot.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-585.jpg)
</Frame>

Next, acknowledge if the service provider has permission to enter the property outside of the Resident's availability, and click **Continue**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-586.jpg)
</Frame>

Lastly, hit **Submit** if there are no other details you want to add.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-587.jpg)
</Frame>

And that's it\! That's how you can update the resident availability through a work order.
