---
title: "How to Override/Change Service Provider Payment Method in Property"
mode: "wide"
description: "Find out how to override or change the payment method for a specific property to ensure your Service Providers are paid correctly."
---

From your Latchel dashboard, click **Places**, then on the dropdown select **Properties**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-759.jpg)
</Frame>

From the property page either **select** a property on the list below or **search** the property that you need to edit.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-760.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Override Service Provider Payment Method**

On the property page that you need to edit, go to the **Payments** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-761.jpg)
</Frame>

From the Payment Settings section, click **Override** located under Service Provider Payment Method.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-762.jpg)
</Frame>

Then on the pop-up window, **select** the payment option that you want to use.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-763.jpg)
</Frame>

And click **Submit.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-764.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Change Service Provider Payment Method**

On the property page that you need to edit, go to the **Payments** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-765.jpg)
</Frame>

Under the Payment Settings section, click the **ellipsis icon** of the Service Provider Payment Method and then select **Change Payment Method**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-766.jpg)
</Frame>

Then on the pop-up window, **select** the payment option that you want to use.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-767.jpg)
</Frame>

And click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/rtaImage-768.jpg)
</Frame>
