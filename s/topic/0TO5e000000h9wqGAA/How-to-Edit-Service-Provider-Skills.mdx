How to Edit Service Provider Skills in the Category and Prioritization Page?


Click on the **three dots** on the right side of the service provider that you want to edit. You will get the option to either **Edit Settings**, **Edit Skills** or **Remove Service Provider from the Priority list**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-854.jpg"/>
</Frame>

Click on **Edit Skills** and you will see a list of skills the service provider has added to their profile.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-855.jpg"/>
</Frame>

From here, you can **check** (add) or **uncheck** (remove) skills. Click **Save** to enter your skill selection. [**_Click here to see How to Navigate Preferred Service Provider Profiles._**](/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wqGAA/How-to-Navigate-Preferred-Service-Providers-Profiles)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-856.jpg"/>
</Frame>

_Please note that checking or unchecking skills will NOT change the service provider's skills in their profile. It will only add or remove skills from the Categories & Priorities page._
