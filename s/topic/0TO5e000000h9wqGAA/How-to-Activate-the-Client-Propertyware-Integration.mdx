---
title: "How to Activate the Client Propertyware Integration"
description: "This guide walks you through the steps to activate the Propertyware integration for your account. By enabling this integration, Latchel will automatically sync with your Propertyware data, ensuring your properties, residents, and work orders stay up-to-date without manual input."
---

To get started, head to your Propertyware dashboard and click on **Setup** located in the top right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Untitled 06 24 2025 01 27 PM Pn](/images/untitled-06-24-2025_01_27_PM.png)
</Frame>

Next, expand the **Administration Setup** located in the left corner, and click on **Manage Users**.

<Frame>
  ![Propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 06 24 2025 01 41 PM Pn](/images/Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-06-24-2025_01_41_PM.png)
</Frame>

Here, we need to create a Latchel user within Propertyware.

To do this, click on **New User** and fill out all of the necessary fields.

<Frame>
  ![Propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 06 24 2025 01 44 PM Pn](/images/Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-06-24-2025_01_44_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![Propertyware On Demand Real Estate Solution 06 24 2025 01 49 PM Pn](/images/Propertyware-On-Demand-Real-Estate-Solution-06-24-2025_01_49_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Check>
  **Make sure to fill out the following data in the form:**

  - **Profile:** Administrative Profile
  - **Status:** Active 
  - **Privilege Level:** Administrator 
  - **First Name:** Team
  - **Last Name:** Latchel
  - **Email:** <u>pwbeta@latchel.com</u>
  - **Password:** Create a password.
  - **Confirm Password: **(retype the password to confirm)
</Check>

After ensuring that all the data is correct and complete, click the **Save** button.

<Warning>
  **Share the password** you created for Latchel, or you can **check the box** for **"Send email for Password reset to User"** for Latchel to reset the initial password you created.
</Warning>

Great job\! You've completed the steps outlined in this guide. 

Now it's time to work with Latchel's Customer Success team to take the next steps. Let your onboarding representative know that you've finished this guide, and they'll be happy to help you with whatever comes next.
