---
title: "How to Assign Service Provider to a Territory / Zip Code"
description: "This capability of managing service providers' assignments based on service territories will significantly reduce the need to repeat and manage service provider setup across various entities (such as Portfolios, and Properties)"
mode: wide
---

You should assign your service provider territory/zip code(s) when you do not want a service provider outside of a property's territory/zip code(s) to be contacted when a work order comes in.

Learn how to assign a service provider to a specific territory/zip code for new and existing service providers.

### **Assigning a specific territory/zip code for a new Service Provider.**

On the Latchel dashboard click **People** then select **Service Provider** on the dropdown.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-841.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Click on **+** **New Service Provider** located on the right side of the service provider page.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-842.jpg"/>
</Frame>


Fill out the service provider information then click **Next**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-843.jpg"/>
</Frame>


After adding the appropriate skills, on the bottom part of the page choose either **Add Territories From Existing Properties** or **Manually Add Zip Codes**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-844.jpg"/>
</Frame>

When you have assigned the territory click **Add** then **Save**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/rtaImage-845.jpg"/>
</Frame>

**FAQs:**
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion
    title="What happens when a Service Provider does not have any zip codes in their service territory list?"
  >
    The Service Provider will be dispatched to all work orders regardless of the location based on service provider prioritization and configuration at the Company, Portfolio, and/or Property level.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion
    title="What if I want to add a Service Provider to a property outside of their area?"
  >
    Latchel console will prompt you to add the property's zip code to the service provider's service territory list. At this point, you could either update their area or abort adding the service provider
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion
    title="What if I want to add a service provider to a portfolio outside of their area?"
  >
    Latchel console will present you with a list of zip code/s within the portfolio not covered by the Service Provider. At this point, you could update their area with one or all of the zip codes that are in the portfolio and not covered by the service provider. _Note: service providers will only be dispatched to the zip codes added to the service provider service territory list._
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
